Understanding Common Wildlife Problems and Signs You Need Wildlife Control

It's important to be aware of the signs that indicate you may have a wildlife problem requiring professional intervention. Early detection can often prevent more significant damage and discomfort. Recognizing these signs is the first step toward a resolution.

Noises in the Attic or Walls

Have you been hearing scratching, scurrying, or thumping noises coming from above your ceiling or inside your walls, particularly at night? These could be indicators of animals like squirrels, raccoons, or even bats making themselves at home. Squirrels are often active during the day, while raccoons and opossums are more nocturnal. The sounds can vary depending on the animal's size and activity level. Ignoring these sounds can allow wildlife to cause significant damage to electrical wiring, insulation, and structural components.

Professional Wildlife Control in Gardena, CA can identify the source of these noises, determine the type of animal, and safely remove it from your property. Leaving these noises unaddressed is not only unsettling but can also lead to costly repairs down the line. Our experts are trained to locate hidden den sites and entry points, ensuring a complete resolution to the noise disturbance.

Unpleasant Odors

A persistent foul odor around your property or emanating from specific areas like the attic or crawl space can be a strong indicator of wildlife presence. These odors can be caused by droppings, urine, nesting materials, or even deceased animals. Skunks are notorious for their potent spray, but other animals can also create unpleasant smells through their waste. These odors are not just a nuisance; they can also pose health risks, especially if the animal is carrying diseases.

Our Gardena Pest Pros team is equipped to locate the source of these odors and address the issue comprehensively. This includes removing the animal, cleaning and sanitizing the affected area to eliminate pathogens and neutralize odors, and implementing measures to prevent future re-entry. Don't live with persistent, unpleasant smells; let us help you restore freshness to your property.

Visible Damage to Structures

Wildlife can cause significant physical damage to your home or business. This can include gnawed wood, damaged insulation, torn wiring, broken vents, or even structural weakening due to nesting and burrowing. Animals like rodents and squirrels constantly need to chew to wear down their teeth, which can lead to damaged electrical wires, posing a fire hazard. Raccoons can tear up roofing materials and siding to gain entry. The extent of the damage can vary greatly depending on the species and the duration of the infestation.

If you notice unexplained damage to your property, it's crucial to investigate whether wildlife is the culprit. Gardena Pest Pros can assess the damage, identify the animal responsible, and develop a plan not only to remove the animal but also to repair the damage and seal entry points to prevent future issues. Protecting your property's structural integrity is a key component of our wildlife control services.

Wildlife can carry various diseases and parasites that pose a risk to humans and pets. Raccoons can carry rabies and roundworm. Bats can also carry rabies. Rodents can spread hantavirus and other bacterial infections. Fleas, ticks, and mites can infest wildlife and then transfer to your pets or even you. Coming into contact with wildlife droppings or urine can also expose you to harmful pathogens.

Gardena Pest Pros takes these health risks very seriously. Our removal and exclusion methods are designed to be as safe as possible, minimizing contact with wildlife. We also offer sanitation services to clean up contaminated areas and reduce the risk of disease transmission.
